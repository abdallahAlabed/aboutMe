String art is one of the most aesthetically pleasing DIY projects you can think of. Often referred to as pin-and-thread art, string art is an addicting artform that is inviting to the most experienced crafter and the newcomer alike. Through the creation of intricate designs, crafters turn a plank of wood into a work of art

The entire concept revolves around a few simple tools and materials:

  • Wood board
  • Embroidery floss
  • Nails
